About
Yoona Yang is a multi-disciplinary designer and media artist, studying and working in Espoo, Finland, born in South Korea. As a media artist who creates artworks with inspiration from nature, she resides at a crossroads between nature and digital media. She tries to find the balance and the coexisting ambivalence - optimistic and pessimistic aspects of media technology - by observing these aspects of everyday life as a starting point for a new artistic practice.
