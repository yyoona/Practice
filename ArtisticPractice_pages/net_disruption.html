Net Disruption / 2015
1 / 7

○ Interactive Sound Installation
○ Dimension: (W) 1000 x (H) 1000 x (D) 1000 m
○ Tools & Materials: OpenFrameworks, MAX/MSP, Metal frame, Iphone, Strings, Speakers

This interactive sound installation offers the visitors a space to interact with this work by using an iPhone and strings.

In the field of interactive media art, artists’ design was to encourage visitors to participate in their artwork voluntarily. This sound installation utilizes playful metaphors such as ‘cat’s cradle’ and musical instruments to help visitors easily recognize the play and act according to the artists’ intention.

○ Showcased in FutureEverything Festival, Manchester City Hall, United Kingdom
○ In collaboration with Hee-eun Kim
