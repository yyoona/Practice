Creepy Smile / 2021
1 / 7

○ Interactive Object
○ Dimension: (W) 135 x (H) 165 x (D) 90 mm
○ Tools & Materials: Arduino, Processing, Plywood, Electronic components

This interactive object is a simple experimental project for the controller box.

Sometimes, when people smile and laugh, it is hard for us to catch the real emotion behind the smile because it includes ambivalent, even more, various emotions. This fact makes the smile creepy.

The project, “Creepy Smile,” encourages the audience can experience creepy emotion with the smile-faced controller box, which has two buttons (eyes) and two slider potentiometers (mouth). While the audience is pressing buttons and controlling the sliders, they can change the color of the divided smiley faces, and also control the laughing and crying sounds.
