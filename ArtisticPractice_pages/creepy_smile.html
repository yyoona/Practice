Creepy Smile / 2021
1 / 7

○ Dimension: (W) 135 x (H) 165 x (D) 90 mm
○ Materials: Plywood, Electronic components

A simple experimental project for a controller box.

Sometimes, it can be difficult to discern the true emotion behind a smile, which may include various, even ambivalent feelings. This can make a smile appear creepy.

The “Creepy Smile” project allows audiences to experience this unsettling feeling using a controller box with a smiling face, featuring two buttons for the eyes and two slider potentiometers for the mouth. As they press the buttons and adjust the sliders, they can change the colour of the divided smiley faces and control the laughing and crying sounds.
