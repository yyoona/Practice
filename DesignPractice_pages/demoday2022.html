Motion poster design for [ Spring Demo Day 2022 ]

The Demo Day is an open gathering of the Aalto Media Lab’s students, alumni, staff and friends. The Spring Demo Day 2022 was held at Design Factory, Aalto University, Finland.

The motion poster is audio visualization work, created with TouchDesigner. The visualized image serves as the main visual asset of the promotion, with two different versions of posters designed: a motion poster and a printed poster. The motion poster is a one minute loop video, specifically created for SNS promotion and the projected background during the Demo Day.

○ Sound by Lucien Montandon for the motion poster.
