Oblivion / 2022

○ Poster: Digital Print / 393 x 545 (mm)
○ Leaflet: Digital Print / 210 x 297 (mm)

Poster and leaflet design for [ Oblivion 2022 ] / Artist: Nam Junghyun

Oblivion is a multi-disciplinary art project that comprises a film screening and a theatre performance, which is screened and performed at different times. While the movie reflects on the past, the performance senses the present and anticipates a meeting with past events.

○ Performance at Arko Arts Theater, Seoul, Korea
○ Film Screening at Emu Cinema, Seoul, Korea
