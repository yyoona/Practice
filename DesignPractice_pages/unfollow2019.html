Visual design lead for [ UNFOLLOW 2019 ]

Unfollow 2019 is an experimental music festival in Paju book city, Paju, Korea.

The visual design for Unfollow includes visual identity design, poster design, application design, web design, SNS promotion, and on-site documentation during the festival.

○ Directed and hosted by Kimchi and Chips and PaTI.
